#2. Grid System - clearfix in Bootstrap.

ClearFix fixes the columns among the cells. Wherever you are feeling the space is odd between the cell apply clearfix.
But, After applying the clearfix, the responsive nature of the screan goes i.e you will see two rows with two columns each, even if the full screen is opened. To fix this, we uses some more classes along with clearfix i.e 'hidden-md' 'hidden-lg'.
If you see in this html page, in div I have provided two classes like col-sm-6 col-md-3. What it means that when the breakpoint happens at the medium level, the cells wont be goint to convert into the four rows(which is usually the case, if I dont use the col-sm-6) instead, these will break into the two rows - each row with two columns having size of 6 columns.
